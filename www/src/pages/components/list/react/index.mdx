## List variations

### Bullet

```jsx
<List>
    <ListItem>item one</ListItem>
    <ListItem>
        item two ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor
        incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
    </ListItem>
    <ListItem>item three</ListItem>
</List>
```

### Number

```jsx
<List theme="decimal">
    <ListItem>item one</ListItem>
    <ListItem>
        item two ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor
        incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
    </ListItem>
    <ListItem>item three</ListItem>
</List>
```
